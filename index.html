Program Overview

This 4-week program targets glute growth and belly fat reduction. All levels supported. Choose 3/4/5 days per week.

Workout Structure

G
Glute Day: Hip thrusts, Romanian deadlifts, Bulgarian split squats, glute bridges, cable kickbacks.
C
Core + HIIT: Planks, mountain climbers, Russian twists, leg raises, 12–15 min HIIT circuits.
A
Accessory: Band walks, clamshells, glute activation drills.

Sample Week (4 days)

  • Day 1: Glute heavy + short core finisher
  • Day 2: HIIT + Mobility
  • Day 3: Glute strength (compound + accessory)
  • Day 4: Active recovery or optional cardio
